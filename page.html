Welcome to EchoVox AI

EchoVox is a state-of-the-art multimodal AI platform designed to bridge the gap between visual text recognition and synthetic speech generation. Our core goal is to provide accurate lip-reading predictions from silent videos and to realistically clone voices for seamless media generation.

Sentence Prediction

Utilizes advanced CNN + LSTM models and MediaPipe face landmarking to accurately read lips from video frames, outputting sentence predictions with high confidence.

Voice Cloning

Integrates leading zero-shot TTS engines (XTTS-v2) to clone a target speaker's voice using only a highly optimal 6-second reference audio sample.
